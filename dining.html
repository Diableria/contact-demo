Fine Dining

Whistler’s culinary scene has evolved into one that rivals cities ten times the size. With more than 170 establishments dishing up delight, Whistler’s range is as big as your appetite. From steak, seafood, sushi, Italian, French, Mexican, quick and tasty to multi-course indulgent! When you’re packing for your Whistler Blackcomb escape, don’t forget to bring your appetite. For the best experience we recommend making reservations in advance.

Christine's On Blackcomb

Welcome to mountain-top dining in style. Enjoy panoramic views, along with classic dishes prepared with the finest ingredients, and a diverse wine list to complement. Enjoy mountain comfort dishes prepared with fresh local ingredients inside or on the sunny patio.

Located at the base of the Creekside Gondola, Dusty’s offers mouthwatering food, arguably the world’s best Caesar, legendary après, party rockin’ live shows and is open everyday 11 a.m. until late.
